To find your <i>Location ID</i>, browse or search for your city from
the <a href="http://weather.yahoo.com/" target="weather.yahoo.com">
Yahoo! Weather</a> home page.  The <i>Location ID</i> is in the URL
for the forecast page for that city.  For instance, if you search for
New York City on the Yahoo! Weather home page, the forecast page for
that city is http://weather.yahoo.com/forecast/USNY0996.html.  The
<i>Location ID</i> is USNY0996.
